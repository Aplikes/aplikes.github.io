\[\int_{}^{} x\ln(x)^2dx\]

\[\int_{}^{} x\ln(x)dx=x^2\frac{ln(x)}{2}-\int_{}^{}\frac{x}{2}dx\]
\[\int_{}^{}\frac{x}{2}dx=\frac{x^2}{2}\]

Відповідь:

\[\int_{}^{}x\ln(x)^2=x^2\frac{\ln(x)^2}{2}-x^2\frac{\ln(x)}{2}+\frac{x^2}{4}+C\]

Або:

\[\int_{}^{}x\ln(x)^2=\frac{x^2(2\ln(x)^2-2\ln(x)+1)}{4}+C\]
